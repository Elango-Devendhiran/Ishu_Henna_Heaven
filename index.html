About the Artist

Where Tradition Meets
Timeless Elegance

For over eight years, Ishu Henna Heaven has been crafting intricate henna artistry that blends royal Indian tradition with refined, modern design. Every design is drawn freehand, shaped around the story of the bride and the moment she's celebrating.

From soft bridal florals to bold Arabic trails, each application uses natural, skin-friendly henna paste for a deep, long-lasting stain.
