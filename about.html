Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Ishu

What began as a childhood fascination with my grandmother's henna cones grew into a full-time craft. Today, Ishu Henna Heaven is a boutique studio dedicated to bridal and celebration mehendi, blending centuries-old motifs with a distinctly modern hand.

Every consultation starts with a conversation — about your outfit, your story, your comfort with detail — before a single line is drawn.

Our Mission

To deliver flawless, personalised henna art that makes every client feel celebrated on their most important days.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted name in luxury bridal mehendi, known for artistry, hygiene and heart.

Our Journey

Experience Timeline

2018

The First Cone

Ishu began freelancing for friends and family, quickly building a reputation for delicate bridal detailing.

2020

Studio Launch

Opened a home studio in Chennai to take dedicated bridal bookings and formal training clients.

2022

200+ Weddings

Crossed 200 bridal bookings, expanding into Arabic and Indo-Arabic specialisations.

2024

Destination Debut

Began travelling for destination weddings across Goa, Udaipur and Kerala.

2026

1000+ Designs

Today, Ishu Henna Heaven has created over 1000 custom designs for 500+ happy clients.
